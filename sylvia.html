Brief:

Sylvia+ is a home decor business established in 2006. Since then Sylvia+ has sold countless decorations and furnitures in bulk to companies instead of selling directly to consumers. This project is part of a brand refresh to establish an online ecommerce prescence.

Problem Statement

With the rise of ecommerce websites such as Amazon or Ebay, it forces out brick and mortar businesses like Sylvia+. The main goal for Sylvia+ is to revitalise the new brand, cut out the middlemen, and build a customer facing mobile app with an optimized purchasing experience from the beginning to end.

Research

The client began by highlighting millenials as the target demographic for appeal with our design. So we reached out to peers that matched the description to learn of their furniture purchasing needs and habits.

Personas

We managed to create two personas from insights gained from our surveys with almost 70 respondents.

Ideation

After defining the problem and gaining insights from research, we began the long process of ideation. We created delivarable documents using various techniques, such as: storyboards, WBS structures, UML and Swim lane diagrams.

Prototyping

Gives the users option to sign in/up or continue as guest, all in one screen.

Home page

Prominently features a carousel giving the user more helpful information while providing Sylvia+ space to showcase products for more discoverability.
