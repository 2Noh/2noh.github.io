Brief:

Sylvia+ is a home decor business established in 2006. At the moment, Sylvia+ sells many different types of designs and furniture in bulk to companies. [our task, the goal, and what we achomplised]

Problem Statement

With the rise of ecommerce websites such as Amazon or Ebay, it forces out brick and mortar businesses like Sylvia+ that sell to companies directly. The main goal for Sylvia+ is to revitalise the new brand, cut out the middlemen, and build a customer facing mobile app with an optimized purchasing experience from start-to-end.

Research

The client began by highlighting millenials as the target demographic for appeal with our design. So we reached out to peers that matched the description to learn of their furniture purchasing needs and habits.

Personas

We managed to create two personas from insights gained from our surveys with almost 70 respondents.

“I like buying modern rustic pieces without leaving the comfort of my own house” - Jessica Barlow, Registered Nurse

Long Beach, CA

“I want to decorate my son’s new room but I don’t have a lot of time.” - Mikey Chen, Finance Advisor

Los Angeles, CA

Insights
Prefers shopping online as long as there is large selections with free shipping and return.

Ideation

After defining the problem and gaining insights from research, we began the long process of ideation. We created delivarable documents using various techniques, such as: storyboards, WBS structures, UML and Swim lane diagrams.
